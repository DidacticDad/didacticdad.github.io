## Democratic

Systematic

A peril some

Will plead

<br>

Democratic

Delight to dump

The right to lead

<br>

Emmigree pedigree

Floating from our

Sinking ship

<br>

With Templeton our

Target, we’re all

Shooting from the hip

<br>

While the water rises…
